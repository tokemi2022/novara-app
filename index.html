Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 · Novara

What we collect
Parent email address, family PIN (stored as plain text in MVP — will be hashed in production), child's name and date of birth, family location (city/country), languages spoken, developmental milestones and moments you choose to log.

Why we collect it
Solely to personalise your child's weekly developmental activity plan and track progress across developmental domains.

Who sees your data
Only you and your co-parent (via your shared family PIN). Novara staff may access anonymised aggregate data for product improvement. We never share identifiable family data with third parties.

Children's data
We are committed to the protection of children's personal data in compliance with GDPR, COPPA, and applicable local laws. We collect only the minimum data necessary.

Data retention
Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request full deletion at any time via Settings → Reset all data, or by emailing support@novara.app.

Security
Data is stored on Supabase (EU-West region, Ireland) with row-level security policies. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Your rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Contact support@novara.app.

For questions: support@novara.app
